The Wild North

Days 1 – 9 • Whitehorse, Skagway, & Juneau

Day 1 • Aug 5

Arrival in Whitehorse

Fly to Whitehorse via Air North. Enjoy dinner at the famous Klondike Rib & Salmon.

📱 Live Flight Watch
Day 2 • Aug 6

Yukon River Kayaking

Embark on a scenic 3 to 4-hour river kayaking excursion covering 24 km of pristine northern waters.

Day 3 • Aug 7

Wildlife & Hot Springs

Visit the Yukon Wildlife Preserve (Bus tours at 11am, 1pm, 3pm) and unwind at the Takhini Hot Springs. Coffee stop at Bean North.

Day 4 • Aug 8

Kluane National Park Hike

A magnificent day trip featuring a 4-hour roundtrip drive and a trek on the stunning Sheep Creek Trail (~6.5-hour hike).

Day 5 • Aug 9

Drive to Skagway

Depart early for wildlife spotting. Route features stops at breathtaking Emerald Lake and the historic town of Carcross.

✓ Overnight: Skagway Booked
Day 6 • Aug 10

Juneau & Mendenhall Glacier

All-day catamaran voyage (8:00 AM - 8:15 PM) to Juneau via the Fjord Express. Explore Mendenhall Glacier before returning.

✓ Fjord Express Booked
Day 7 • Aug 11

White Pass Train Journey

Take the iconic 3.5 to 4-hour White Pass Scenic Train ride, then drive 2.5 hours back to Whitehorse late for optimum wildlife viewing.

Days 8–9 • Aug 12-13

Open & Flexible Days

Aug 12: Boating with Matt, open trails, or alternative hikes.
Aug 13: Packing and final evening dinner gathering.

The Pacific Coast

Days 10 – 14 • Vancouver, Tofino, & Victoria

Day 10 • Aug 14

Vancouver Arrival & Suspension Bridge

Fly from Whitehorse to Vancouver. Pick up the airport rental car, explore Capilano Suspension Bridge, and head to The Sandbar at Granville Island for dinner.

Day 11 • Aug 15

Ferry & Road Trip to Tofino

Catch the 2-hour ferry from Horseshoe Bay to Departure Bay (Nanaimo). Road trip to Tofino via Coombs (Goats on the Roof) and Cathedral Grove.

Meet Up with Krystle & Jobsts
Day 12 • Aug 16

Whale & Bear Watching Tour

An early wildlife cruise starting at 9:00 AM (Check-in 8:30 AM). Spend the afternoon exploring beaches.

✓ Overnight: Ucluelet Booked
Day 13 • Aug 17

Victoria Heritage & Gardens

Enjoy a scenic 4-hour coastal drive to Victoria. Spend the afternoon strolling The Butchart Gardens and visiting Hatley Castle.

✓ Overnight: Sidney Booked
Day 14 • Aug 18

Return Journey to Toronto

Board the 1.5-hour Swartz Bay to Tsawwassen ferry, return the rental car, and catch your WestJet flight home.
